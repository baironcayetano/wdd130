Bairon Cayetano | WDD 130

Hello! My name is Bairon Cayetano, and I am from Honduras. I am twenty-one years old; I love playing soccer and I enjoy speaking English, even though my mother tongue is Spanish.

I served a full-time mission in Belize for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, where I shared the Gospel of Jesus Christ in English. This experience greatly helped me develop my language skills

I have always liked computers and have been learning about them since I was sixteen; I truly feel I can do amazing things with them. I decided to learn programming because when I wrote my first 'Hello World' program, I discovered I could give instructions to a computer and it would follow them. That opened up a world of possibilities to create things that can help and improve the lives of others.

Currently, I am studying Web Design and Development at BYU-I through BYU-Pathway, and I am excited to keep learning. On this page, you will be able to see my progress.
